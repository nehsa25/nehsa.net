About Well Life Medicine: Well Life Medicine is a leading primary care and mental health clinic dedicated to providing top-notch healthcare services. We are committed to enhancing patient care through innovation and efficiency. We currently use AthenaHealth as our EHR system and Phreesia for patient check-ins and medical questionnaires.

Role Overview: We are seeking a talented and self-motivated Software Engineer to join our team. The ideal candidate will have a strong background in automation, system integration, and a passion for improving healthcare processes through software development. This role involves working closely with the business owner and healthcare providers to identify and solve problems using technology.

Key Responsibilities:

Develop and implement solutions to enhance or replace Phreesia integration, improving data flow into AthenaHealth.
Create software to automate handling of appointment cancellations, no-show filling, and confirmations.
Develop systems for annual tracking and scheduling automation.
Implement AI-driven auto-responses for patient communications.
Build and manage website messaging and patient interaction platforms.
Generate and automate report pulling for various needs.
Set up automated reminders and notifications via calendar or Slack.
Create software to automate AthenaHealth schedule updates and billing processes.
Develop tools for scheduling and billing audits.
Automate pre-filling of templates and billing information in AthenaHealth.
Build systems to pre-screen bills and auto-review denials.
Automate payment posting and management of quality measures.
Develop tools to scrub, report, and alert for quality measure gaps.
Manage ticklers and automate tracking of patient reviews and HR onboarding processes.
Qualifications:

Proven experience as a Software Engineer, ideally in healthcare or related fields.

Proficient in automation tools, AI, and web development.
