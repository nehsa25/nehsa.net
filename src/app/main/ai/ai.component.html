  • Working with Prompts Tips and tricks for working with prompts in AI.
    • Be specific. Tell me about australian cattle dogs and how they differ from other breeds will get you better result than Tell me about dogs
    • What tone and perspective are you looking for? Write a 3 sentence horror story about Timothée and Gaston. Reply as a retired Appalachian sewist visiting Utah. is better than Write a 3 sentence horror story about Timothée and Gaston.

      Gemimi Answer:
      Well, I never did much care for them fancy city folk names, Timothée and Gaston. Seemed like something out of a fairy tale, all fancy and whatnot. But when them two showed up in my little Utah town, I knew somethin' was amiss. Their eyes, they was hollow, like lookin' into a bottomless well. And then the folks started disappearin', one by one, just like they was swallowed by the earth.
