Pi, like quadratic equations, was a mystery to me. Although I learned the calculations, I had no understanding of what they meant or how they would be used in real-world scenarios.

Unfortunately, the list of topics I'm not strong on is long—I wouldn't be the go-to person for Ships, Cars, Space travel, or most things. Still, I want to be educated and always strive to learn more.

That's why it makes me happy when a small piece of the universe snaps into place; for me, it was with pi.
